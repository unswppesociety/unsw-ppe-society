Our Mission

The UNSW Politics, Philosophy, and Economics Society is dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity and critical thinking among students interested in these interconnected disciplines.

We provide a platform for students to:

  • Engage in thoughtful discussions about current affairs and philosophical questions
  • Connect with like-minded peers and build a supportive community
  • Access valuable resources for academic and career development
  • Hear from industry leaders, academics, and thought leaders
  • Develop critical thinking and analytical skills

What is PPE?

Politics

Understanding power, governance, and the structures that shape our societies and international relations.

Philosophy

Exploring fundamental questions about ethics, knowledge, reality, and the human condition through critical reasoning.

Economics

Analyzing how societies allocate resources, make decisions, and create value in an interconnected world.
